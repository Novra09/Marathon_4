About Natsukawa Aika

Appearance

Aika is considered to be beautiful and very cute in appearance (as pointed out by many people in the story) with upper-back length bright orange hair that has square bangs she keeps open at school with a side braid on the left side and emerald-green eyes.

Personality

Aika appears to be a cute, diligent and friendly girl who is adored by her classmates. She is hardworking and gets frustrated on not being able to do good enough. She often comes off as harsh when dealing with Wataru Sajou but is actually kind and caring. She never used to be interested in matters regarding love and thus finds it difficult to understand and deal with romantic stuff.

Background

middle school, Aika's parents used to be busy with their jobs and their financial condition wasn't very well. Seeing their hardships, Aika took it upon herself to do the household work and taking care of her little sister which gained her a dedicated, diligent and hardworking personality. She studied meticulously and got enrolled in a prestigious high school called Kōetsu High where she eventually got followed by Wataru Sajou.

About Sajou Wataru

Appearance

Wataru has black hair (currently painted a dark brown) that has bangs swept to the left and purple eyes. In general, his appearance is considered average.

Personality

Initially at the start of the story, Wataru appears to a pushy, dedicated and clingy existence to Aika. But after waking up to reality, he starts to show a laid-back attitude and a mature side of him. He ceases to be the source of laughter and entertainment of his class and he tries to stay away from any unwanted attention, However, due to his devotion and love for Aika, he makes quite assertive decision at times in order to help her. Wataru also demonstrates traits of social acumen and experience. Throughout the series he helps with some of his schoolmates' personal problems, showing his perspective on the subject and giving advice to help them. This characteristic earned Wataru recognition from people like Rin Shinomiya and the other members of the Disciplinary Committee.

Background

Since middle school, Wataru has always admired Aika. He confessed his love to her countless times but always ended up in rejection. He studied hard to catch up to her and get enrolled in the same high school as her, Kōetsu High. This continued until when one day while chasing her on the way to school, he nearly got hit by a soccer ball which woke him up from his fantasy and made him realize he is not worthy and decided to give up on her.
